## 📋 Technical Specifications (3-Layer Excel Design)

### Layer 1: Job Listings
Columns: Company | Job Title | 104 Link | Core Requirements | Salary Range | Location | Post Date

### Layer 2: Candidate Profiles  
Columns: Name | ID | Current Role | Years Experience | Core Skills | AI Score | Stability % | Talent Level | LinkedIn/GitHub

### Layer 3: Matching Analysis
Auto-calculated columns:
- `符合度%` = skill match formula
- `穩定性評估` = personality + stability factors
- `推薦優先度` = priority ranking (S/A/B)
- `開發狀態` = development stage tracking
